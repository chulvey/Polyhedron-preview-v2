How this section is meant to be used

This framework is written so a new player can enter through simple language and leave with stronger tools for real multiplayer games. It is also meant to reward experienced players by naming patterns that already exist in serious Commander play and organizing them into a repeatable system.

The deck pages show those ideas in action. The framework pages explain why they work.
